The World of Die Letzte Seite

Overall Story

Synopsis

In the library of Kette exists a story tampered by magic. Memories fade through the ink incomprehensible by human kind. The ending of which lies hidden within the pages of a certain book.

Suddenly "You" awake inside Kette of which feels like home yet not. With unknown yet familiar corridors, dimly lit hallways and neverending rooms. You found a book that bears your name and a page that looks like the void itself.

As "You" venture around the place, you met allies but also enemies you have yet to know... and then suddenly — "Seek the Last Page... Rewrite your fate." Words that echoed through the hallways. The clues that tie to the fate of the world and the last page that awaits.
