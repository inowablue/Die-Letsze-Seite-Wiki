STORYLINE

The World of Die Letzte Seite

Overall Story

Synopsis

In the library of Kette exists a story tampered by magic. Memories fade through the ink incomprehensible by human kind. The ending of which lies hidden within the pages of a certain book.

Suddenly "You" awake inside Kette of which feels like home yet not. With unknown yet familiar corridors, dimly lit hallways and neverending rooms. You found a book that bears your name and a page that looks like the void itself.

As "You" venture around the place, you met allies but also enemies you have yet to know... and then suddenly — "Seek the Last Page... Rewrite your fate." Words that echoed through the hallways. The clues that tie to the fate of the world and the last page that awaits.

The book that bears your name is incomplete. Its final page is missing — torn, erased, or perhaps never written at all. And as the line between reader and character begins to blur, a question lingers: Are you rewriting the story… or simply fulfilling it?

Chapters

Chapter-by-Chapter Breakdown

Chapter I Introduction — The First Twilight
[ Chapter I Image ]
Chapter I — Key Scene
 The Awakening

You awaken within the endless halls of Kette — a place that feels both unfamiliar and strangely nostalgic. The silence is overwhelming, broken only by the faint rustling of pages echoing through the corridors.

As you explore, you discover a grand library filled with countless books, each one seemingly alive. Among them, one stands out — a book that bears your own name. Inside, its pages recount events you cannot remember, yet somehow feel real.

The deeper you venture, the more the world reacts to your presence. Doors unlock, shadows linger, and whispers guide — or mislead — your path. The chapter ends with a realization: you are not merely inside the story… you are part of it.

Chapter II Murder — Ink and Blood
[ Chapter II Image ]
Chapter II — Key Scene
 The Crime

The fragile calm of Kette is shattered when a body is discovered within the library. Ink stains the floor like blood, and the once silent halls are filled with suspicion and fear.

The inhabitants — each with their own secrets — begin to turn on one another. Clues are scattered across pages, hidden in dialogue, and buried within fragmented memories. Nothing is as it seems, and every answer only raises more questions.

As tensions rise, you are forced to investigate. Who can be trusted? Who is hiding the truth? And most importantly… is the story itself trying to deceive you?

Chapter III Witch Trial — The Accused
[ Chapter III Image ]
Chapter III — Key Scene
 The Trial

Accusations reach their peak as a trial is declared. Within a chamber beyond logic, the rules of reality bend — and truth becomes something that must be proven, not assumed.

Testimonies clash, contradictions surface, and fragments of magic intertwine with logic. Each character defends their innocence, while evidence begins to point toward a hidden culprit.

Chapter IV Ending — The Last Page
[ Chapter IV Image ]
Chapter IV — Key Scene
 The Final Choice

At last, the final page awaits. The truth has been uncovered — or perhaps constructed — through every choice you have made.

The world of Kette begins to reshape itself depending on your decisions. Allies may remain, enemies may fall, and the story reaches its iconclusion.

But one question remains: what is the "Last Page"? Is it an ending… a beginning… or something that transcends both? In the end, the pen is in your hands — and the fate of the story is yours to write.
